Holding Court Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Holding Court is a basketball coaching app developed by Adrianne Harlow.
This policy describes what information the app collects and how it's used.

Information We Collect
- Email address: used to create and access your account
- Workout data: drills, sessions, player roster, and statistics you create
- Account identifier: a unique ID used to associate your data with your account

How We Use Information
We use this information solely to provide the app's features: storing your
data securely, syncing it across your devices, and authenticating you when
you sign in. We do not share, sell, or rent your information to third
parties. We do not use your data for advertising or analytics.

Data Storage
Your data is stored using Google Firebase services. Authentication is
handled by Firebase Authentication and data is stored in Cloud Firestore.

Voice Recognition
Holding Court uses your device's microphone for hands-free workout
tracking. Audio is processed by Apple's speech recognition service to
detect commands like "make" and "miss." Audio is not stored by Holding
Court and is only processed while the voice feature is actively turned
on during a workout.

Children's Privacy
Holding Court is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights
You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by
emailing the contact below. We will respond within 30 days.
